MERIT Lab

"MERIT Lab: Empowering Innovation and You through Microelectronics"

MERIT (MicroElectronics based on Remarkable Integrated circuits Technology) Lab was established in 2023 at The Catholic University of Korea. The name MERIT also reflects our hope that all members of the lab will develop their own strengths and grow through research. We focus on the design and implementation of semiconductor-based integrated circuits and miniatured systems for biomedical and environmental applications. By combining low-power analog and mixed-signal circuit design with sensor integration, wireless communication, and wireless power transfer technologies, we aim to create intelligent platforms that unify the monitoring of human health and environmental conditions.

Through this integrated approach, we pursue innovative system-level solutions that contribute to a healthier, safer, and more sustainable future. Our research spans from circuit-level innovations to full system integration, enabling us to develop practical and scalable solutions for real-world use. We also work closely with experts from other disciplines to help bring our technologies closer to real-life impact and accelerate their translation into meaningful applications.
