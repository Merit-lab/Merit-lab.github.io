    2023 하계방학 대학생 실무특강 참석 2023.08.23-25

    Most of the researcher students participated in the 2023 Summer Vacation College Student Practical Training Special Lecture hosted by 'Nine Plus Academy'.
    As for the educational content, I took a special lecture on Schematic & Layout using Cadence Virtuoso. Through this, I had the opportunity to practice Full-Custom IC design.
